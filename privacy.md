# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date**: November 20, 2025

## Application Overview

FastRecon (WriteUpBot) is an internal accounting automation tool used by Young & Associates for reconciliation of investment account statements with QuickBooks accounting records.

## Data Collection

FastRecon accesses the following data from QuickBooks Online:
- Chart of Accounts (account names and hierarchical structure)
- Account types and classifications

**No financial transaction data, balances, or customer information is accessed from QuickBooks.**

## Data Usage

Data retrieved from QuickBooks is used exclusively for:
- Matching securities and accounts in Morgan Stanley investment statements
- Generating journal entries for import into QuickBooks
- Internal accounting reconciliation processes

## Data Storage

- QuickBooks OAuth tokens are stored locally on the user's computer
- Chart of Accounts data is cached locally for reconciliation purposes
- All data files are stored on internal secure servers
- **No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties**

## Data Sharing

**No data is shared with third parties under any circumstances.**

All financial data remains confidential and is processed only within Young & Associates' internal systems.

## Data Security

- OAuth tokens are stored securely and excluded from version control
- Users must maintain secure access to their workstations
- All data access follows QuickBooks OAuth 2.0 security protocols
- Application access is restricted to authorized employees only

## Data Retention

- OAuth tokens are stored until manually deleted or expired
- Chart of Accounts cache is refreshed as needed for reconciliation
- No historical data is retained beyond current reconciliation needs

## User Rights

Authorized employees may contact the system administrator for:
- Questions about data handling
- Requests for data removal
- Access to stored credentials
- Privacy policy clarifications

## Third-Party Services

FastRecon integrates with:
- **QuickBooks Online** (Intuit Inc.) - Chart of Accounts access only
- **Morgan Stanley** - Statement data (provided manually by user, not API access)

## Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated as needed. Users will be notified of any material changes.
